About Our Platform

Interactulum is dedicated to creating meaningful connections between alumni and students. Our platform provides opportunities for alumni to mentor students, share insights from their professional experiences, and offer valuable career advice.

Alumni can reconnect with peers, offer mentorship, and contribute to the growth of future professionals. Students benefit from access to a network of experienced professionals, receive guidance, and enhance their career prospects.

Join us in building a dynamic community where every interaction fosters success and opportunity.
